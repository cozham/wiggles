About Me

Hi my name is Kyle! Welcome to this page that's about me.

Kyle was born in Fort Collins, Colorado in the late ninenties. After spending five years in Bulgaria as a kid, he developed a love for world travelling and an inability to sit still. After his family moved back to Colorado when he was in 5th grade, Kyle decided that craft breweries and the great outdoors were not really his cup of tea.

Thanks to his love of Norah Ephron movies and a love for the, he decided to move to New York City in 2015 to attend Marymount Manhattan College. After realizing that the Upper East Side school wasn't for him, he decided to transfer to The New School where he is currently a Junior studying Journalism and Design. Kyle loves a lot of things including interior design, fried rice, female folk artists from the 1970s, and reality television. He lives in Bushwick in a charming prewar two bedroom, a steal for it's size and location!

Kyle is not quite sure what he plans on doing with his life, but wants it to be something artistic and outside the world of 9 to 5 jobs. He loves the idea of working in television, but will never in 1,000,000 years move to Los Angeles.

Those are a few key facts about Kyle!
